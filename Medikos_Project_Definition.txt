# MEDIKOS HEALTH COMPASS - PROJECT DEFINITION & REFERENCE

## PROJECT OVERVIEW
Medikos Health Compass is a React-based healthcare web application providing patients with AI-powered health management tools, medical document storage, and natural health recommendations, with dedicated dashboards for doctors and administrators.

## SYSTEM PURPOSE
To provide a digital healthcare platform where patients can manage their health data, receive AI-powered health insights, and maintain comprehensive medical records, with role-based access for healthcare providers.

## CORE STAKEHOLDERS (ACTUAL IMPLEMENTATION)

### 1. PATIENTS (Primary Users - Full Implementation)
- Dashboard with health metrics (Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, BMI, Sleep)
- AI-powered infectious disease prediction based on symptoms
- Generic medicine alternatives with price comparisons
- Health diary with mood tracking and analytics
- Natural remedies and diet suggestions based on symptoms
- MediVault for secure medical document storage and management
- AI chatbot for health queries and guidance
- AI voice agent for voice-activated health assistance
- Digital health passport with QR code generation
- Profile and settings management

### 2. DOCTORS (Secondary Users)
- Doctor dashboard with patient analytics and health metrics overview
- "My Patients" section with comprehensive patient management
- Access to patients' Health Passport for complete medical history review
- Review of patients' AI-predicted diseases and risk assessments  
- Health Diary analytics showing patient mood patterns and behavioral insights
- Access to shared documents from patients' MediVault with permission-based viewing
- Patient communication and consultation scheduling
- AI-assisted diagnosis support and treatment recommendations

## ACTUAL FUNCTIONAL MODULES (CURRENT IMPLEMENTATION)

### PATIENT PORTAL FEATURES (FULLY IMPLEMENTED)
1. **Dashboard**: Health metrics display (Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, BMI, Sleep), quick actions grid, recent activities
2. **Infectious Disease Prediction**: Symptom selection interface, AI-powered disease prediction with probability percentages
3. **Generic Medicine Suggester**: Medicine search, alternative suggestions with pricing and manufacturer details
4. **Health Diary**: Entry creation with mood/category selection, calendar view, analytics dashboard with trends
5. **Natural Remedies**: Symptom input, herbal remedies suggestions, diet recommendations (include/avoid lists)
6. **MediVault**: Document upload with categorization (Prescription, Report, Insurance, Vaccination, X-Ray), file management
7. **AI Chatbot**: Interactive health consultation interface (/chatbot route)
8. **AI Voice Agent**: Voice-activated health assistance (/voice-agent route)
9. **Health Passport**: Personal health profile with emergency contacts, medical history, QR code generation
10. **Profile & Settings**: Account management, AI preferences, privacy settings

### DOCTOR PORTAL FEATURES
1. **Doctor Dashboard**: Patient analytics dashboard with detailed health metrics and trends
2. **My Patients**: Comprehensive patient management with health status monitoring
3. **Patient Health Passports**: Access to complete medical profiles and history
4. **Disease Prediction Review**: View patients' AI-generated health risk assessments
5. **Health Diary Analytics**: Patient mood patterns, behavioral insights, and trend analysis
6. **Shared Documents Access**: Review medical documents shared by patients through MediVault
7. **AI Diagnosis Assistant**: AI-powered diagnostic support and treatment recommendations
8. **Patient Communication**: Secure messaging and consultation scheduling



## CURRENT TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

### FRONTEND IMPLEMENTATION
- **Framework**: React 18.3.1 with TypeScript
- **Build Tool**: Vite v5.4.1 for development and build process
- **Routing**: React Router v6.26.2 with role-based navigation (patient/doctor/admin dashboards)
- **State Management**: React Context API (AuthContext for user authentication)
- **UI Framework**: shadcn/ui components built on Radix UI primitives
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom gradient themes and medical designs
- **Animations**: Framer Motion v12.23.22 for page transitions and interactions
- **Icons**: Lucide React v0.462.0 for consistent iconography
- **Forms**: React Hook Form with Zod validation

### DATA MANAGEMENT (CLIENT-SIDE)
- **Authentication**: Frontend-only simulation with role-based routing
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage for user preferences and temporary data
- **File Handling**: File uploads with metadata management (MediVault)
- **State Persistence**: Context-based state management across components

### ROUTING STRUCTURE
- **Public Routes**: `/` (Landing), `/login`
- **Patient Routes**: `/dashboard`, `/remedies`, `/medivault`, `/passport`, `/generic-medicine`, `/disease-prediction`, `/health-diary`, `/voice-agent`, `/chatbot`, `/settings`, `/profile`
- **Doctor Routes**: `/doctor-dashboard`
